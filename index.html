About Me

I’m a fourth-year Computer Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University, with a strong interest in building systems that sit at the intersection of hardware and software.

My technical focus areas include embedded systems, real-time applications, and full-stack development. I enjoy designing solutions that are clean, intentional, and built to last, whether that’s through microcontroller-based projects or modern web platforms.

Outside of engineering, I spend a lot of my time playing and streaming video games, staying active through basketball and fitness, and exploring new ideas through personal projects. I’m always looking to learn, improve, and take on challenges that push me beyond my comfort zone.
